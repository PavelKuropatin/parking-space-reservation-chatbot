## Booking Process

Reserving a space takes about two minutes.

1. **Search** – Open the CityPark app or website and enter your **arrival date, time, and duration**.
2. **Choose a space** – Select a space type (Standard, EV, Oversized, etc.). Availability and price are shown in real time.
3. **Enter vehicle details** – Provide your **license plate number**; this enables automatic barrier entry.
4. **Pay** – Confirm and pay securely. You'll receive a booking confirmation and QR code by email and in the app.
5. **Arrive & park** – Drive up to the barrier. The camera reads your plate (or scan your QR code at the reader) and the gate opens automatically.
6. **Exit** – On departure, the barrier opens automatically if you're within your booked window. Overstays are charged at the drive-up rate.

### Managing Your Reservation

- **Extend:** Add time from the app, subject to availability.
- **Modify:** Change date/time up to 1 hour before start (price difference may apply).
- **Cancel:** Free cancellation up to 1 hour before start; full refund to original payment method within 3–5 business days.

## Questions & Answers

**Q: Do I need to print my reservation?**
A: No. Entry is automatic via license-plate recognition. The QR code in the app is a backup if the camera can't read your plate.

**Q: What if I arrive earlier or later than my booked time?**
A: You can enter up to 15 minutes before your start time at no charge. Arriving late doesn't extend your end time — the booked window stays the same.

**Q: Can I change the license plate after booking?**
A: Yes. Edit the plate in the app up to 1 hour before your arrival.

**Q: What happens if the facility is full when I arrive?**
A: A confirmed reservation guarantees a space. If a space is somehow unavailable, staff will direct you to an alternative bay or a nearby partner facility at no extra cost.

**Q: How do I get a receipt or invoice?**
A: Receipts are emailed automatically. Download a VAT invoice anytime from *My Bookings* in the app.

**Q: Is there a charge for cancelling?**
A: No, as long as you cancel at least 1 hour before your start time. Later cancellations are non-refundable.

**Q: Can I reserve an EV charger separately from a parking space?**
A: No — EV bays include both the space and the charger. Select an *EV Charging* space when booking.

**Q: What if my plate isn't recognized at the barrier?**
A: Press the help button at the barrier or scan your QR code at the reader. Support staff can open the gate remotely 24/7.

**Q: Are oversized vehicles like vans or campervans allowed?**
A: Yes, on Level 6 (2.50 m height limit). Book an *Oversized / SUV* space to ensure a wide enough bay.

**Q: Is the facility accessible at night?**
A: Yes, it operates 24/7 with on-site security and lighting on every level.
